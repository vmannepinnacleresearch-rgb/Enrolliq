The Problem

Clinical recruitment
is flying blind

Sites run on fragmented systems, inconsistent workflows, and gut instinct. The data exists — it's just locked in silos, overwritten, and impossible to act on.

🗂️
Status history thrown away
Most CTMS platforms store only the current status. You can't answer "where were patients last month" because that data is gone. Every transition overwrites the last.
👤
No unified patient identity
A patient referred to Study A and Study B exists as two separate records. No institutional memory. No cross-study visibility. Duplicated work at every step.
📊
Analytics require days of manual work
Leadership asks "how many leads do we need for Study X?" and a data engineer spends 3 days pulling from 4 systems to answer a question that should update automatically.
🔀
Every site does it differently
No standardization means no comparability. You can't benchmark Site A against Site B when they're each tracking recruitment in their own ad-hoc way.
3 days

Average time a data engineer spends answering "how many leads do we need to hit enrollment?" — a question that should answer itself.

We built Enrolliq because we lived this problem. Not as consultants — as data engineers inside clinical research sites, watching coordinators work around systems that were supposed to help them.

How It Works

One system. Complete visibility.

Built around a simple but powerful idea — the patient is the center of everything. Every study, every interaction, every data point traces back to one profile.

01 / Capture
🎯

One Patient Profile. Forever.

Every lead enters as a universal patient record — not a study record. When they're referred to a second study six months later, you see their full history: who contacted them, what they said, why they didn't enroll before. No duplicate data, no lost context.

02 / Track
📋

Append-Only Status History

Every status change is recorded — never overwritten. You can see that a patient was "Contacted" on March 3rd, "Scheduled" on March 10th, and "No Show" on March 15th. The full timeline is always there. This is the data that makes analytics possible.

03 / Analyze
🔮

Intelligence, Not Reports

Enrollment forecasts update in real time. The system knows your conversion rates, your outreach velocity, your screen fail patterns — and it tells you what you need to do before leadership has to ask. No exports. No pivot tables. Just answers.

Full Feature Set

Everything a site needs.
Nothing they don't.

Designed with and for clinical research coordinators — the people who actually do the work.

🧠
Enrollment Predictor

AI-powered forecasting tells you exactly how many leads you need to hit your enrollment target, based on your actual site's conversion history — not industry averages.

👤
Universal Patient Profile

One record per patient across all studies and sites. Full interaction history, visit log, status timeline, and study branches — all in one place, never duplicated.

📊
Live Funnel Dashboards

Real-time visibility into every stage of your recruitment pipeline, filterable by site, study, date range, referral source, and coordinator. Always current, no refresh needed.

🔗
CTMS Integration Ready

Built with an open API architecture designed to connect to CRIO, REDCap, Veeva, and any CTMS platform. Enrolliq sits as the recruitment intelligence layer — compatible with whatever clinical system you already use.

📡
Referral Source Intelligence

Track which referral sources bring the best patients — not just the most leads. See conversion rates, screen fail rates, and cost-per-enrollment broken down by every source you use.

⚡
Speed & Response Metrics

Measure time-to-first-contact, contact-to-screen velocity, and coordinator response rates. Identify where leads are going cold and intervene before they drop out of the pipeline.

🧪
Pre-Screening Engine

Configurable eligibility questionnaires per study. Coordinators run through inclusion/exclusion criteria at first contact — stopping unqualified leads before they take up appointment slots.

💬
Integrated Communications

Send SMS and email directly from the patient profile. Every message sent is logged automatically to the interaction timeline — no copy-pasting into a separate CRM.

🏥
Multi-Site Network Control

Network admins see everything. Site admins see their sites. Recruiters see their patients. Role-based access that matches how your organization actually works, not how software vendors think it works.
